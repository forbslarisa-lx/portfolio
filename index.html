About Me

I specialize in translating complex knowledge into clear, usable learning.
My work combines instructional design, pedagogy, and knowledge translation. Across projects, I focus on transforming complex academic or technical content into structured learning experiences that remain accurate while becoming accessible to diverse audiences.

This work takes different forms depending on the context. In some projects, it means translating research-based or theoretical material into public-facing learning programs. In others, it involves adapting technical or operational knowledge so that it can be understood and applied by multilingual teams working in engineering or high-risk industries.
Across both contexts, the core challenge is the same: preserving the integrity of complex knowledge while making it understandable, structured, and applicable in real-world situations.
