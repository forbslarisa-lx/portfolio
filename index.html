About Me

I'm Larisa Forbs, a Learning and Capability Development professional with years of experience designing and delivering learning programs across education and corporate environments. My work combines strong foundations in pedagogy and adult learning science with hands-on instructional design, from curriculum architecture and instructor-led training to eLearning development, SCORM packaging, and LMS deployment.
A significant part of my work focuses on technical and multicultural learning environments, where training must translate complex operational knowledge into clear, structured learning experiences. I design training that helps technical professionals understand procedures, communicate effectively, and apply knowledge in real operational contexts.
My experience includes working with professionals in engineering and high-risk industries as well as multilingual teams with varying levels of English proficiency. In these environments, training must balance technical accuracy, safety awareness, and communication clarity so that processes and instructions remain understandable and actionable for diverse teams.
Across projects, I focus on designing learning that supports practical application and operational performance, integrating structured learning frameworks with opportunities for practice, reflection, and feedback.
